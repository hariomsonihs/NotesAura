Refund & Shipping Policy

Refund Policy

Since NotesAura provides digital study materials (downloadable notes), returns are not applicable once the product is delivered to your account. However, refunds are granted in specific cases:

  • Duplicate payment made for the same order.
  • Technical error during payment processing resulting in no access to the purchased notes.
  • Unsuccessful or failed transactions verified by Razorpay.

Refunds, if approved, will be processed automatically to your original payment method within 7-10 working days (subject to bank processing time).

Late or Missing Refunds

  • Check your bank account again.
  • Contact your credit card company; posting may take time.
  • Contact your bank, as processing delays are common.
  • If still unresolved, email us at support@notesaura.com.

Sale Items

Refunds apply only to regular-priced purchases. Discounted or promotional purchases are non-refundable.

Exchanges

Exchanges are not applicable for digital products. If you face access issues, contact us at support@notesaura.com for resolution.

Gifts

If the product was marked as a gift at purchase and delivered to you, refunds (if applicable) will be provided as credit to your NotesAura account.
